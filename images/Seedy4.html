The U.S. Has Been Trying To Get Ahold Of The Man Who Created The Internet For A While Now

"We've been trying to reach out to the man who created the internet for weeks now, but every time we get close, his phone mysteriously disconnects from the Wi-Fi," said a frustrated government official. "It's like he has some kind of firewall protecting him from us. We even tried sending him a friend request on MySpace, but he never responded. It's like he doesn't want to chat with us about the latest cat memes or conspiracy theories. We just want to thank him for all the endless hours of procrastination and fake news he has given us."
