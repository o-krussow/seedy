The U.S. Military Has Released A List Of Things It Would Like To See In A Movie

"We would really love to see a movie where the hero takes out the bad guys using only a drone strike from his couch while eating Cheetos," said a spokesperson for the U.S. military, as they unveiled their list of desired movie scenarios. "Oh, and it would be great if the protagonist could also defuse a bomb with just a paper clip and some chewing gum. And of course, there has to be a dramatic scene where they walk away from an explosion without looking back. That's essential." The military also emphasized their interest in seeing a plotline where the villain is defeated by a highly trained team of military dolphins.
